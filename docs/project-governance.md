# Project Governance Scaffolds (Sanitized)

## Purpose
Lightweight templates for planning, ownership, and compliance alignment.

## Sizing (Pizza Scale)
- Slice: <= 1 hour
- Small: half day
- Medium: 1-2 days
- Large: 3-5 days
- Stack of boxes: 1-2 weeks
- Whole restaurant: ~1 month
- Entire strip mall: multi-month program
- Pizza planet: multi-quarter transformation

## RACI Template
Define who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed per work item.

| Work Item | R | A | C | I |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: alert routing | Security Eng | SOC Lead | Net Ops | Stakeholders |

## Stakeholder Analysis (Power/Interest)
| Stakeholder | Goals | Concerns | Power | Interest | Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: SOC Lead | Visibility | Alert fatigue | High | High | Weekly |

## SWOT (Strengths / Weaknesses / Opportunities / Threats)
| Strengths | Weaknesses | Opportunities | Threats |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: Centralized logs | Limited storage | Tuning automation | Noise creep |

## PESTLE (External Factors)
| Political | Economic | Social | Technological | Legal | Environmental |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: Policy change | Cost controls | Analyst burnout | New SIEM features | Compliance updates | Power outages |
